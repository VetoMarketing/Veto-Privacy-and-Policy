Privacy Policy for Veto Chrome Extension
----------------------------------------

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

1. Data Collection
------------------
Veto collects Instagram profile URLs and first names from pages you actively browse in Chrome. 
This data is stored only in the Google Sheet you connect to. 
Veto does NOT send any data to our servers.

2. Data Usage
-------------
All data collected is used solely to help you manage influencer outreach. 
Veto does not use your data for marketing, analytics, or any other purposes.

3. Data Sharing
---------------
We do NOT share, sell, or distribute your data under any circumstances.

4. Data Deletion
----------------
You control your data. You can delete your Google Sheet at any time, which permanently removes all collected information.
